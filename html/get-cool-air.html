Should I Replace Or Repair My Air Conditioner?

There’s a few signs to look out for that’ll tell you if you need to replace or repair your unit. Here are a few signs your AC unit needs to be replaced:

  • Bills are higher than they used to be
  • Your air conditioner has trouble keeping you cool
  • AC unit is 10 years or older
  • Lack of cool air & limited airflow
  • Frequent breakdowns
  • AC uses R22 freon (it is phased out by the federal government)

If you are experiencing any of these signs, contact Active Plumbing & Air Conditioning today! We offer next-day installations.
